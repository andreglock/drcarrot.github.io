Ecology for Microbiome Management

I study how microbiomes form over time, and how they rearrange
following disturbances. By understanding what makes microbes tick,
we may become better at managing them.

Synthesis in microbial ecology

In collaboration with Jon Chase, Nico Eisenhauer and Ashley shade, I am using the wealth of sequencing data already available to derive general principles of community assembly for microbes.
